Why Computer Science?

I have very faint memories of my past in the Philippines, but one aspect that seems to still be vivid to this day is my journey to the United States at the age of 5. It was only my third time meeting my father and I was about to venture to a foreign land. My first introduction to the technology of the west was a Gameboy Advance that my dad gave me as a welcoming gift. The planes, the cars, and the moving trains weren't what fascinated me, it was playing Legend of Zelda Link to the Past on this tiny but powerful Computer. Ever since I've always been intranced with every peice of device that comes to light every year and the software that releases along side it. It wasn't until junior year of highschool where I fully realized all of the complexeties of software, the foundation Alan Turing set for all of Computer Science, the importance of the boolean, and so much more. I went to college to learn more about Computer Science and how to create clean software.
